Markets covers research and analysis of commercial real estate sectors and specific
geographic markets. Articles in this category synthesize institutional research findings
from trade publications and financial media on office building supply and demand, retail
network expansion strategies, and regional commercial property dynamics. Market summaries
reference only factual findings — occupancy trends, announced expansions, sector indicators —
sourced and cited from the institutional research record.
