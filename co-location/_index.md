Co-location is the methodology for identifying and ranking commercial real estate sites
based on the presence, density, and composition of anchor tenants. Articles in this
category cover the anchor taxonomy, floor plate analysis, key plan geometry, site scoring,
and the spatial vocabulary used to evaluate and compare buildings across markets.

This category includes the terminological foundation for how buildings are measured,
subdivided, and compared — from Building Owners and Managers Association (BOMA)
standards to proprietary floor-plate subdivision classifications.
