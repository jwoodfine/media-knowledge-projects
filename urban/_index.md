Urban covers the demographic, mobility, and economic forces that shape commercial real
estate demand at the city and regional scale. Articles in this category address commuter
patterns, population distribution, urban fringe dynamics, gateway city characteristics,
and the relationship between demographic concentration and commercial anchor demand.
Understanding urban context informs site evaluation at every scale, from metropolitan
trade area definition to individual block-level analysis.
