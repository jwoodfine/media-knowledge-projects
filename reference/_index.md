used across the projects knowledge base. Articles in this category define the vocabulary
of commercial real estate, building science, AEC industry conventions, and geographic
analysis as used in this knowledge base. Sourced from the canonical projects glossary
(`glossary-projects.csv`) and from AEC / BOMA / BICSI industry standards.
